## Stages 🚀

- 0 (Strawman) - rough ideas, no criteria
- 1 (Proposal) - solve a existing issue
- 2 (Draft) - initial spec
- 3 (Candidate) - feedback, implementations
- 4 (Finished) - ready for inclusion
